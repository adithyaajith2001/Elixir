Services

For Doctors

You can view a patient’s entire medical history in one place, making it easier to provide the best care without asking for unnecessary tests or scans. You’ll have quick access to all previous reports, medications, surgeries, and treatments right at your fingertips—so you can focus more on what matters: treating your patient.

For Patient

Say goodbye to carrying physical medical records to every hospital or clinic. Elixir stores all your medical information in one secure location, making it easy for you and your healthcare providers to access your complete history anytime. Share your records with any hospital or specialist, ensuring you get the care you need without the hassle of managing paper records.

For Laboratory

Labs can directly upload lab reports and images to a patient’s profile. This makes it easier for both patients and doctors to access important information without delays. By adding results directly into the app, you ensure everything is in one place—streamlining communication and improving care for everyone involved.

About Us

At Elixir, we believe that healthcare should be simple, seamless, and accessible for everyone. Our app was built with a clear mission: to bridge the gap between patients, doctors, and labs by providing an integrated platform that brings everything together. Elixir was created to make managing health easier for all parties involved. By digitizing health records, streamlining communication, and simplifying access to information, Elixir aims to provide better care for patients while empowering doctors and labs with the tools they need to make informed decisions.
